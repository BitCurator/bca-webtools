BitCurator Access WebTools

The bitcurator-access-webtools service allows you to browse raw and forensically-packaged (.E01 and .AFF) disk images. Supported file systems include FAT, ExFAT, NTFS, HFS+, EXT2/3/4, ISO 9660 (CD-ROM), and YAFFS2 (Android). Click on 'Browse' to examine partitions and file systems, or 'Download' to download the complete disk image.
