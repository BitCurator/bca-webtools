BitCurator Access WebTools

The bitcurator-access-webtools service allows you to browse raw and forensically-packaged (.E01 and .AFF) disk images. Supported file systems include FAT12, FAT16, FAT32, NTFS, HFS+, EXT2/3/4, and ISO9660. Click on 'Browse' to navigate through the file system(s) within the disk image, or 'Download' to download the complete disk image.
